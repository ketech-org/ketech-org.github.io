Need help with any of our mobile apps? We’re here.

**Primary support email:** [support@ketech.org](mailto:support@ketech.org)

When you email us, please include (if possible):

- The app name and platform (iOS / Android).
- Your device model and OS version.
- A brief description of the issue and steps to reproduce.
- Screenshots or screen recordings, if relevant.

### Using the “App Support” link in the App Store

If you’re viewing one of our apps in the Apple App Store, tapping **App Support** will bring you here so you can reach us directly at **support@ketech.org**.

### Response times

We aim to reply within 2 business days. For urgent issues affecting accessibility or payments, we prioritise responses as quickly as we can.
